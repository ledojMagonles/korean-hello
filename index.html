ABOUT

One night in a neighbourhood, our group of friends discovered our mutual love for Korean food during a casual dinner party. We talk about the tantalizing flavours of korean street foods, and it has ignited a shared passion within us. We decided to turn our love for Korean cuisine into something more than just shared dinners. We huddled around the dining table, brainstorming ideas and forming a plan.

Our journey began humbly with a food stall at a local weekend market. With a modest but carefully curated menu of Korean favourites, we served hungry market-goers and people who love korean foods. Our mouthwatering dishes quickly gained a reputation, drawing crowds to our stall. Word spread like wildfire, and we soon had loyal customers. Our close bond helped us navigate the challenges of running a business. We celebrated the successes, learned from our mistakes, and made sure to keep our love for Korean cuisine at the heart of everything we did.

As months passed, our popularity soared, and we decided to open a cozy little Korean eatery in the heart of the neighborhood. Our physical store was decorated with touches of Korean culture and provided a welcoming space for diners to enjoy Korean street foods. And now we have created this website for you to inquire about our business and our menu.
